Top 3 Romantic Restaurants in Boston for Date Night

1. Contessa
- Location: 3 Newbury St., Back Bay, Boston
- Why it's romantic: Buzzy rooftop extravaganza with checkerboard tiles, striped curtains, and lush plant life - feels like a greenhouse designed by Marie Antoinette and Donatella Versace
- Cuisine: Northern Italian-inspired with exceptional dishes like pillowy ricotta gnudi, Mediterranean branzino, and grand bistecca Fiorentina
- Special feature: Sweeping views of the Public Garden from the second-floor dining room
- Contact: 617-536-5700, contessaristorante.com

2. Bistro du Midi
- Location: 272 Boylston St., Back Bay, Boston
- Why it's romantic: Second-floor dining room with sweeping views of the Public Garden, perfect for gazing into each other's eyes
- Cuisine: Provençal-inspired with sumptuous beef tartare, New England seafood bouillabaisse, and dark chocolate soufflé
- Special feature: Elegant tasting menu with optional wine pairings available
- Contact: 617-426-7878, bistrodumidi.com

3. Deuxave
- Location: 371 Commonwealth Ave., Back Bay, Boston
- Why it's romantic: Fine French-American restaurant famous for hosting wedding proposals (with rings delivered inside sugar spheres)
- Cuisine: Elegant plates like foie gras with poached quince, spiced duck breast with persimmon puree, and swoon-worthy wine list
- Special feature: Painterly plates and sophisticated atmosphere suitable for celebrating milestones
- Contact: 617-517-5915, deuxave.com

Bonus Romantic Options:
- The Beehive: Cabaret stage and bohemian-style enclave with live jazz
- Mamma Maria: North Square 19th-century townhouse with white tablecloths and skyline views
- Oleana: Eastern Mediterranean kitchen with intoxicating aromas and fairy-like garden patio

Note: All restaurants are located in Boston proper or nearby neighborhoods, making them ideal for a memorable date night experience.
